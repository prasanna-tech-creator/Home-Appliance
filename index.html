FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer home service for appliance repairs?
Yes! We offer doorstep service across Tiruppur and nearby areas. Book a service call via phone or WhatsApp and our certified technician will visit your home at your preferred time slot.
Are the spare parts you use genuine?
Absolutely. We use only 100% authentic OEM and manufacturer-approved spare parts sourced directly from brand distributors. All replaced parts come with warranty.
What brands do you service?
We service all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Godrej, Bosch, Haier, Panasonic, Voltas, Daikin, and more. If you don't see your brand listed, call us — we likely service it.
How quickly can a technician reach my home?
For urgent requests, we aim for same-day service within Tiruppur city limits. Scheduled appointments are available the next day for outstation customers.
Do you offer an Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)?
Yes, we offer comprehensive AMC plans for all home appliances. Contact us to get a customized quote based on the number and type of appliances you own.
Can I pay online or by UPI?
Yes! We accept all payment methods — cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and bank transfers. EMI options are also available for large purchases.
